There is also a Gitter chat for QuickMyAnimeList as well, available here! Simple general questions on Gitter, complicated ones on Reddit.

Logged In Yet?

It seems like you haven't logged into your MyAnimeList account yet. QMAL is like, 100% dependent on MAL, (even the name says it), so you basically HAVE to login to your MAL account to see through all the features and have a fun and easy life.
Click on "Credentials" at the navigation bar at the top, and login.
